Garlic Bread Recipe

Ingredients

  • ½ cup butter, softened
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • ¼ teaspoon sage
  • 3 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 French baguette, halved lengthwise
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese, or to tastee

Directions

  • STEP 1

    Preheat oven to broil.

  • STEP 2

    In a medium bowl combine butter, mayonnaise, sage, garlic, oregano, salt and pepper.
    Spread mixture evenly on bread and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.

  • STEP 3

    Place bread under broil for 5 minutes, or until lightly toasted.
